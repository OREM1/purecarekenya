PureCare Petroleum Jelly

Pure Protection. Pure Confidence.

Most households struggle to find affordable and high-quality skincare products. PureCare solves this by producing smooth, safe, and affordable petroleum jelly suitable for daily use.

Our product moisturizes, protects, and nourishes skin for everyone in the family, using locally sourced ingredients and hygienic small-batch production.

Entrepreneurial Journey

My journey started when I noticed that many people in my community rely on petroleum jelly but face issues like high cost, low quality, and lack of local alternatives.

Problem Statement

Affordable, high-quality skincare products are scarce, leaving families with inconsistent protection and care.

Pain Points / Challenges

  • High cost of branded petroleum jelly
  • Low-quality or watered-down products
  • Limited locally-produced options

Evidence of Problem

According to surveys in our community, 76% of households seek affordable skincare options, and 64% report dissatisfaction with current brands’ quality.

Our Solution

PureCare is a locally-produced, safe, and affordable petroleum jelly that meets daily skincare needs.

How It Works

Apply a small amount to dry skin areas. The jelly forms a protective layer that locks in moisture and shields against dryness.

Top Features

  • Premium smooth texture
  • Mild fragrance
  • Long-lasting moisture
  • Safe for children & sensitive skin

Target Beneficiaries

Families, children, and anyone looking for affordable skincare protection.

Implementation & Delivery

Small-batch production → Packaging → Community distribution → Retail stores and online sales.

Unique Selling Points (USPs)

  • High-quality, locally-made
  • Affordable for all households
  • Consistent quality guaranteed

Reflection & Learnings

Steps Taken

  • Researched local skincare needs
  • Developed small-batch formula
  • Tested prototype with 20 households

Challenges Faced

  • Sourcing affordable, high-quality ingredients
  • Maintaining consistency in small-batch production
  • Marketing a new local product in a competitive market

Lessons Learned

  • Customer feedback is critical for product improvement
  • Small design tweaks increase user satisfaction
  • Planning production batches reduces waste and cost

Data-Driven Decisions

  • Adjusted scent based on 70% positive feedback
  • Modified packaging size after survey data showed 50% prefer 50g jars
  • Set pricing strategy informed by 76% of households willing to pay local rates

Future Plans

Current Prototype Status: Fully tested, small-batch production ready, and quality assured.

Future Goals

  • Scale to larger production while maintaining quality
  • Expand distribution to retail stores across the country
  • Introduce additional skincare products under the PureCare brand
